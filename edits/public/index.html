Guest Checkout Reports

Rate behavior, not just stays.

A blind mutual-reveal ledger for property hosts. Hold guests accountable with a credit-check system for short-term rentals.

VERIFIED STAY 100% BLIND REVEAL

Most platforms leave hosts flying blind. You take on the risk of property damage, rule-breaking, and poor communication without a reliable way to vet guests beforehand.

How the Ledger Works

01

Declare the Stay

After a booking concludes, file the record in the system.

02

Private Rating

Both host and guest submit their behavioral reports independently.

03

Simultaneous Reveal

Reports remain sealed until both sides submit, ensuring honest feedback without retaliation.

Standardized Metrics

CLEANLINESS

Condition of the property compared to check-in.

PROPERTY CARE

Damage or unauthorized changes to the space.

RULE COMPLIANCE

Adherence to smoking, pets, and guest limits.

COMMUNICATION

Responsiveness and honesty during the stay.

HOUSE RESPECT

General care for stated rules and checkout times.

Built on Integrity

The blind mutual-reveal mechanism is the core of the platform. By sealing reviews until both parties submit, we eliminate the fear of retaliatory ratings. If a score is inaccurate, the built-in dispute process allows a moderator to review both sides fairly.
